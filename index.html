What is Contentment?

🌱

Contentment is a state of peaceful happiness and satisfaction with one's life, possessions, and circumstances.

"He who is not contented with what he has, would not be contented with what he would like to have."

— Socrates

  • ✓ Peaceful acceptance of present
  • ✓ Growth with gratitude
  • ✓ Joy in the journey
  • ✓ Internal satisfaction
  • ✓ Active appreciation
VS

Complacency

  • ✗ Passive indifference
  • ✗ Avoiding challenges
